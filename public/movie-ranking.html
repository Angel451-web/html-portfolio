The best movies according to Angel

My top 3 movies of all-time.

How the Grinch stole christmas

This is my all-time favorite, especially during the holiday season.
I watch it almost every Chrismas, and it never fails to make me feel all cozy and nostalgic.
There's just something heartwarming about watching the Grinch's heart grow three sizes.

Shrek

Its silly, funny, smart and has more heart than you'd expect. A comfort movie for sure.

Bad Boys

A really great action, comedy movie that never gets old.
